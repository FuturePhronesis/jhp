A Northern Cardinal
sits on a fence and sings his song.

From the Sibley Field Guide - "Song a series of high, clear, sharp, mostly slurred whistles woit woit chew chew chew chew chew or pichew pichew tiw tiw tiw tiw tiw tiw; many variations. Call a high, hard tik; also a softer, rising twik."
